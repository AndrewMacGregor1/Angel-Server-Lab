### **1. Phase 2: Home Lab Utility**

- **Network-Wide Ad-Blocking (Pi-hole or AdGuard Home):**
    
    - **Concept:** Block ads at the DNS level for every device on your home network.
        
    - **Educational Goal:** Learn about DNS records, DHCP settings, and local IP management.
        
- **Personal Cloud Storage (Nextcloud):**
    
    - **Concept:** A self-hosted alternative to Google Drive for your files and photos.
        
    - **Educational Goal:** Managing persistent data volumes and database containers.
        
- **Custom Service Dashboard (Homarr or Heimdall):**
    
    - **Concept:** A clean web interface to launch all your "Angel Server" apps without typing ports.
        

### **2. Phase 3: Storage Expansion & Hardware Upgrades**

- **The SSD/HDD Migration:**
    
    - **Concept:** Moving the OS or high-speed containers to a larger SSD while using the 500GB HDD for bulk storage (like backups or media).
        
    - **Educational Goal:** Learning about Linux disk mounting, `/etc/fstab`, and Proxmox storage pools.
        
- **Resource Monitoring (Uptime Kuma):**
    
    - **Concept:** Real-time monitoring of your services with phone notifications if Minecraft or NPM goes down.
        

### **3. Phase 4: Media & Content**

- **Automated Media Server (Plex or Jellyfin):**
    
    - **Concept:** Streaming your own media library to your phone or TV.
        
    - **Educational Goal:** Hardware passthrough in Proxmox and managing large media libraries.
        

### **4. Long-Term Infrastructure (CCNA Focus)**

- **Physical Networking with Cisco:**
    
    - **Concept:** Integrating a physical Cisco switch into the lab environment.
        
    - **Educational Goal:** Hands-on practice with VLANs, trunking, and port security to prep for the CCNA exam.
